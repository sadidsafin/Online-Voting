About This Election

Bangladesh General Election 2026

The Bangladesh General Election 2026 is a landmark democratic exercise involving over 1,100 registered constituencies across the nation. Citizens elect representatives to the Jatiya Sangsad (National Parliament) through a first-past-the-post voting system. This digital platform enables secure, real-time vote submission and transparent result tracking.

🏛️

Jatiya Sangsad

The national parliament of Bangladesh consists of 300 directly elected seats. Members serve 5-year terms and are responsible for legislation, budget approval, and government oversight.

🗺️

Constituencies

Bangladesh is divided into 300 constituencies across 8 administrative divisions — Dhaka, Chittagong, Rajshahi, Khulna, Barisal, Sylhet, Rangpur, and Mymensingh.

⚖️

Voting System

Bangladesh uses a first-past-the-post (FPTP) system. Each voter casts one vote for a candidate in their constituency, and the candidate with the most votes wins the seat.

🔒

Secure & Transparent

This system uses voter ID verification, one-vote-per-voter enforcement, and real-time result tracking to ensure integrity and public confidence in the democratic process.
